# Overview

This repository contains work on the mechanical modeling of a periodic bundle of two coupled fibers and the estimation of its effective Young's modulus under different boundary-condition assumptions and parameter settings, which I carried out during my engineering studies at [Centrale Lille](https://centralelille.fr/en/) in collaboration with [IEMN](https://www.iemn.fr/en/) / [LIMMS](https://limms-tokyo.org/) as part of the [SMILL-E project](https://smmil-e.com/about/overview/) in 2015.
